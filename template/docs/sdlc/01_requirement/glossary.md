# Glossary

Single source of truth for domain terms. Every term used in the SRS appears here with exactly **one** definition.

## Rules

- One term, one definition. If two meanings appear, split into two terms.
- Prefer the business term over the technical one.
- Link acronyms to full form.
- Update here before introducing a term in any other doc.
